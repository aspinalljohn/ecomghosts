Common questions

How long until I see results?

Most clients see increased engagement within 2-3 weeks. Meaningful pipeline impact typically shows in 60-90 days as we build consistency and authority. LinkedIn rewards consistency over time, not one-off viral posts.

What if the voice doesn't match?

We build a custom style guide in the first two weeks through interviews and analysis of your existing content. You get unlimited revisions on every post until it sounds exactly like you. Voice match is non-negotiable.

How much input do I need to give?

Initial setup requires 2-3 hours (strategy call, voice capture, content audit). After that, 30-60 minutes per week for feedback and approvals. We handle research, writing, formatting, and scheduling.

Can I pause or cancel?

Yes. Monthly plans can be paused or cancelled with 30 days notice. We recommend a minimum 90-day commitment to see compounding results, but you're not locked into an annual contract.

What's the turnaround time?

Posts are delivered 3-5 business days before scheduled publish date. Rush requests (24-48 hours) are available on Growth and Scale plans for timely topics or breaking news in your industry.

Do you write for industries outside eCommerce?

No. We only work with eCommerce founders, Amazon operators, agency leaders, and commerce SaaS teams. This specialization is why the content works—we speak the language and understand the landscape.
