Common questions

+How long until I see results?

Most clients see increased engagement within 2-3 weeks. Meaningful pipeline impact typically shows in 60-90 days as we build consistency and authority.

+What if the voice doesn't match?

We build a custom style guide through interviews and content analysis. You get unlimited revisions until it sounds exactly like you. Voice match is non-negotiable.

+How much input do I need to give?

Initial setup requires 2-3 hours. After that, 30-60 minutes per week for feedback and approvals. We handle research, writing, and formatting.

+Can I pause or cancel?

Yes. Monthly plans can be paused or cancelled with 30 days notice. We recommend a minimum 90-day commitment to see compounding results.

+What's the turnaround time?

Posts are delivered 3-5 business days before scheduled publish date. Rush requests available on Growth and Scale plans.

+Do you write for industries outside eCommerce?

No. We only work with eCommerce founders, Amazon operators, and commerce SaaS teams. This specialization is why the content works.
